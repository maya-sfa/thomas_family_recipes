Zest of one orange
Juice from one orange
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 C confectioner's sugar
Pinch salt
1 - 3 Tbsp milk or cream

Place all ingredients except the milk or cream in a bowl. Whisk
together. Add milk or cream until desired consistency is achieved. Use
immediately.
