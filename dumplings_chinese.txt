Makes 16 dumplings

1 oz (28g) of chives, chopped small
12 oz shrimp
1 inch ginger, shredded
1/2 tsp tapioca starch
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp fish sauce
1/4 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp oil

Cut the shrimps into small pieces (about 3-4 pieces per shrimp), add
chives and mix well. Marinate the filling with the rest of the
ingredients for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.

Place 1 tsp of filling per wrapper. Set fillings on pre-cut parchment
paper.

Steam until wrapper is transparent, 8 - 10 minutes.
