Make chicken_stock.txt, having removed any spare meat from the chicken
caracass and reserved it.

Soup:

1 lbs carrots, peeled and sliced
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 onions, chopped
7 cloves garlic, minced
Stock from above
Leftover chicken from carcass
1/4 C fresh parsely, chopped

Dumplings:

1 1/2C all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg, beaten
1/3 C milk

Heat a stock pot. Add olive oil, and sweat onions and celery until
onions are translucent, about 7 minutes.

Add the garlic, and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

Pour stock into stock pot. Add carrots.

Bring to a boil, and cook for 15 minutes. While cooking, make Dumping
dough:

Mix together dry ingredients. Add wet ingredients, and stir with a
silicone spatula until just combined.

When the 15 minutes are up,  drop by the (cutlery) teaspoon into the
soup, and cook until done, 10 - 12 minutes.

Add the reserved chicken and parsely. 

Serve immediately.
