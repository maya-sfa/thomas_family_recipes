2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
28 oz tomato puree [trying with just chopped, not drained]
16 oz dried black beans
1 medium red onion, chopped
12 garlic cloves, minced
3 Tbsp chili powder
1 Tbsp cocoa powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp smoked paprika
1/4 tsp red pepper
2 cans Guiness beer

Put it all in the slow cooker, cover, and cook on low for 8 hours (or
high for 6)
